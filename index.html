Hope for Children in Myanmar

Totals-Inc is a UK-based charity dedicated to protecting and supporting children affected by the civil war in Myanmar — providing aid, education, and a path to a better future.

About Us

Totals-Inc was founded with a single mission: to ensure that no child in Myanmar is left without support, safety, or hope. The ongoing civil conflict has displaced millions of families, leaving children without access to food, shelter, healthcare, or education.

We work with local partners on the ground to deliver emergency relief, build safe learning environments, and provide long-term community support for the most vulnerable children across conflict-affected regions.

Based in London, we operate with full transparency and are committed to ensuring every pound donated reaches those who need it most.
